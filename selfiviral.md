3. Converting Images to Video using AI
This is the tricky part you there are Free and Paid version available for this.

> Go to Google Flow: https://labs.google/fx/tools/flow/

> Click “Create with Flow” button

> You may need to sign up using Gmail account or any other email.

> Once logged in and inside the Google Flow. Select “Frame to Video” from the prompt box.


Choose Frame to Video
> Upload starting 1st image, choose portrait mode > click Crop and Save

> Use it as first frame.


Choose Portrait Mode
> Upload 2nd image after first is done and use as second frame.

> Paste below prompt in Google flow and it will generate the video for 2 frames.

the men having mobile in hand walks into first scene and takes selfie then same men with mobile runs the other side into the other frame and takes selfie with the other character.  no fading, no dissolving, no background warping. Should look real. mobile should always in hand of the same person and not in any other character hand. no dialogue. mild smile always.

Final flow screen
> You can follow the same process to generate multi videos with other celebs. 

> Keep in mind to use same 2nd image as 1st image in flow, when generating next transition video.

> Download each video file and merge while posting on Instagram or in any editing software.
